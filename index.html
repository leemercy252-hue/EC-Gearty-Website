About Our Firm

A Legacy of Trust &
Legal Excellence

E. C. Gearty & Company Solicitors has served County Longford and surrounding areas since the late 1980s. Founded by Enda Gearty and now led by Managing Partner Frank Gearty, the firm has built a reputation for professional legal representation, practical advice, and strong community connections.

The firm combines decades of legal experience with modern legal expertise across property law, litigation, family law, wills and probate, local authority law, and public administration matters.
